# Product Idea

Working product name: FixMySEO

Build an SEO tool for non-coders, website makers, small business owners, and digital marketers.

The tool checks a user's website for SEO issues and helps fix those issues with one-click actions, so users can get more traffic with less effort, less time, and a lower cost than hiring expensive SEO help.

## One-Sentence Version

I am building an SEO tool for non-coders, website makers, small business owners, and digital marketers so they can find and fix website SEO issues quickly without paying high prices or spending too much effort.

## Core Promise

Check my website, show what is hurting my traffic, and help me fix it fast.

## First User

Small business owners with simple websites.

They care about getting more traffic, usually cannot afford expensive SEO agencies, and need plain-language fixes instead of technical SEO reports.
